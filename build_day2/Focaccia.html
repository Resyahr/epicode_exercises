Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart camberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cramberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thingly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not neccesary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen Cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
